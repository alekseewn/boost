Numeric Constants

Overview

Contains all constants provided by C++20’s <numbers> specialized for the decimal types. These do not require C++20.

Symbol

Value

e_v

Euler’s Number

log2e_v

log2(e)

log10e_v

log10(e)

pi_v

pi

inv_pi_v

1/pi

inv_sqrtpi_v

1 / sqrt(pi)

ln2_v

ln(2) where ln is the natural log

ln10_v

ln(10)

sqrt2_v

sqrt(2)

sqrt3_v

sqrt(3)

sqrt10_v

sqrt(10)

inv_sqrt3_v

1 / sqrt(3)

cbrt2_v

cbrt(2)

cbrt10_v

cbrt(10)

egamma_v

Euler–Mascheroni constant

phi_v

The Golden Ratio

There are also non-template variables that provide the constant as a decimal64_t type.
